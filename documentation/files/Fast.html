Fast


The "Fast" meter button applies the "Fast" time response with a time constant of τ =0.125 seconds.


The "fast" time response is usually used when a source has a high dynamic range and fairly quick variations in the temporal response.  There is much less averaging and smoothing than with the "slow" time response.


The fast time response is explained in more detail in ANSI S1.4-1983.
