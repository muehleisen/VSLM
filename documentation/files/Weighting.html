Weighting


The Weighting buttons: "A", "C" and "Flat" apply the "A" weighting, "C" weighting as defined by ANSI S1.42-2001

or no weighting at all.


The weighting filters are custom designed digital filters


There are three weighting's available


A Weighting


C Weighting


Flat Weighting


Reference:

ANSI, S1.42-2001 (R2006)  American National Standard Design Response of Weighting Networks for Acoustical Measurements: American National Standards Institute, 2001.
